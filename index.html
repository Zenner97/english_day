How to practice

The idea is simple: you don't memorize rules — you drill the phrases you actually need until you can say them before you even think.

  • 1List. Your phrases about your day — the foundation of everything.
  • 2Listen. Put it on in the background and loop it until the sound feels natural.
  • 3Shadow. Say each phrase out loud at the same time as the speaker, at the same speed.
  • 4Recall. Read the Russian, say the English yourself, then check.
  • 5Speak. Put it all together into a real story about your day.
Plan for today (you've got one day):
• Morning — Listen for 20–30 min in the background + skim the List.
• Afternoon — Shadow for 15 min, then a first round of Recall.
• Evening — Recall until everything's green + Speak it out loud 2–3 times.
